Our Commitment

At Nerd Hive, we believe that celebrating nerd culture should be accessible to everyone. We're committed to ensuring our website provides a welcoming, inclusive experience for all visitors, including those with disabilities.

We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards across our website.
