Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

At Nerd Hive ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website nerdhiveofficial.com or make a purchase from us.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address: When you subscribe to our newsletter or join our mailing list
  • Order information: When you make a purchase (name, shipping address, payment details)
  • Communications: When you contact us with questions or feedback

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time and date of your visit
  • General location (city/region level)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send you order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Send newsletter updates about new product drops (only if you've subscribed)
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and products
  • Comply with legal obligations

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our mailing list, you'll receive:

  • New product announcements and launches
  • Exclusive early access to new designs
  • Occasional promotions or discounts
  • Behind-the-scenes content and updates

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send, or by contacting us directly. We will promptly remove you from our mailing list.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we do our best to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Printify: Our print-on-demand partner who fulfills and ships orders
  • Stripe: Secure payment processing
  • Formspree: Email collection for our newsletter

These partners have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your information. We encourage you to review their policies.

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your email address with other companies for marketing
  • Send spam or excessive promotional emails
  • Store your payment card information (handled securely by Stripe)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Opt out of certain data collection

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
